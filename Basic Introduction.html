TTL Coin: That's The Labor

A Novel Blockchain Solution for Workforce Contracts

Introducing TTL Coin

TTL Coin (That's The Labor) is a unique utility token designed to revolutionize workforce contracting. It provides interest-free liquidity to companies for labor contracts and dynamically adjusts token supply through a unique issuance and burning mechanism, aiming for value stability.

Interest-Free Liquidity

Companies access funds without interest for labor contracts, significantly reducing financial burden.

Dynamic Supply Control

Token supply adjusts based on real economic activity (labor contracts), ensuring market stability.

Transparent Contracts

Blockchain ensures visibility and immutability of labor agreements, fostering trust and accountability.

TTL Coin Ecosystem Flow

The TTL Coin mechanism operates on a unique cycle of issuance and burning, directly tied to the lifecycle of labor contracts on the blockchain. This innovative approach ensures a real-economy driven token supply and value.

1. Company Requests Labor Contract

A company registers a labor contract on the blockchain and initiates a request for TTL Coin issuance for payment.

2. Contract Valuation & Approval

The TTL governance/Coin Issuer Node evaluates contract validity and labor value (e.g., via oracles), then approves.

3. TTL Coin Issuance & Loan

TTL Coins equivalent to the approved contract value are issued as an interest-free loan directly to the company's designated account.

4. Company Executes Contract & Uses TTL

The company utilizes the loaned TTL Coins to compensate labor and fulfill the contract obligations.

5. Contract Completion & Loan Repayment

Upon successful verification of contract completion, the company repays the previously loaned TTL Coins.

6. TTL Coin Burning

Repaid TTL Coins are immediately sent to a burn address, permanently reducing the total network supply.

Key Considerations

Implementing this innovative model requires careful planning and robust solutions for several critical areas to ensure its long-term success and integrity.

Area Description
Labor Value Valuation Establishing objective and fair criteria for converting labor contract value into TTL Coins (e.g., hourly rates, project complexity, market averages).
Contract Non-Compliance Defining clear smart contract logic for penalties (e.g., collateral burning, credit score reduction) and coin recovery/burning in case of contract non-compliance by either party.
Governance Model Implementing a transparent and decentralized governance model (e.g., DAO, voting system) for critical decisions like coin issuance approval, supply adjustment, and valuation criteria changes.
Initial Supply & Distribution Carefully planning the initial circulating supply and distribution method for TTL Coins at mainnet launch to prevent drastic market fluctuations and ensure a fair start.
